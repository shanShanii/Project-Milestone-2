Climate Change

News Flash: As soon as you step outside, you will burn to ashes - If we don't do anything about the current state of climate change, this could happen in a few years taking place on Earth, our planet. Climate Change is the long-term change in temperatures and weather patterns. Both internal and external factors contribute to this. As greenhouse gas emissions blanket the Earth, it traps the sun's heat. This would eventually lead to Global warming, which would have a major impact on all of us.

Factory pollution is one of the greatest factors at causing Climate Change. It may look like it consists only of smokestack emissions but can also include acidifying rain, chemical spills, greenhouse gases and toxic waste disposal.

Effects of Climate Change

Look at the poor Polar Bear losing its home. Due to global warming, the ice melted and now they have to suffer for our actions.

Have you ever wondered why at the morning it's so hot while at the afternoon, it rains so hard? This is also due to climate change.

Are you ready to help prevent further effects of Climate Change to us?

  • Make Your Community Green -

    • Plant trees and plants at your garden!

  • Be More Conservative

    • Cutdown on energy usage in your household. Make sure to turn off lights and unplug devices if you are not using it anymore.

  • Recycle!

    • Collect plastic, glass or anything that can be of use and turn it into materials again.

  • Advocate Everyone

    • Let them know about these tips! If everyone will be alarmed and be educated about Climate Change, it can absolutely help to the environment. Remember the famous quote, "The more, the merrier."
